Rules and Guidelines

Welcome to Doinik Bangla Kagoj! To ensure a respectful and constructive environment for all users, we have established the following rules and guidelines. Please read them carefully.

1. General Conduct

  • Be respectful to other users. Harassment, bullying, or any form of abusive behavior will not be tolerated.
  • Do not post or share content that is offensive, discriminatory, or harmful, including but not limited to hate speech, racism, sexism, or violence.
  • Keep discussions civil and constructive. Avoid personal attacks or derogatory remarks.

2. Content Guidelines

  • Do not post or share false or misleading information. All news and comments should be factual and verifiable.
  • Avoid posting spam, advertisements, or irrelevant content.
  • Do not share explicit or inappropriate content, including images, videos, or text.
  • Respect copyright and intellectual property rights. Do not post content that you do not have permission to share.

3. Commenting Rules

  • Comments should be relevant to the news article or topic being discussed.
  • Do not post comments that are off-topic, repetitive, or intended to provoke others.
  • Refrain from using profanity or offensive language in comments.
  • Users must be logged in to post comments. Anonymous commenting is not allowed.

4. Account and Security

  • Do not share your account credentials with others. You are responsible for all activity on your account.
  • Do not attempt to hack, exploit, or otherwise compromise the security of the website or other users’ accounts.
  • If you suspect your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at [email protected].

5. Consequences of Violations

  • Violations of these rules may result in warnings, temporary suspension, or permanent banning of your account, depending on the severity of the violation.
  • Inappropriate content will be removed without notice.
  • Repeated violations may lead to legal action if they involve illegal activities.

6. Reporting Issues

If you encounter any content or behavior that violates these rules, please report it to us at [email protected]. Provide as much detail as possible, including screenshots if applicable.

By using Doinik Bangla Kagoj, you agree to abide by these rules and guidelines. We reserve the right to update these rules at any time, and it is your responsibility to stay informed of any changes.
